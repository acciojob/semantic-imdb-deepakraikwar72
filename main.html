Summary

*Inception* is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a professional thief who specializes in stealing secrets by infiltrating people's dreams. He is offered a chance to have his criminal record erased if he can successfully perform "inception"—planting an idea into someone’s mind.

Actors

  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
